A few words about us

Strategic, Cost Effective Asset Maintenance Solutions

Our target: To be your preferred partner for your Asset Maintenance requirements

Our mission at Optima is to deliver cost-effective solutions without compromising quality. Accurate and up to date data is crucial to the management of Asset Operations and without confidence in the data within in your Enterprise Resource Management (ERM) database – it’s easy for the wrong decisions to be taken.


At the heart of Optima's philosophy is a belief that is focused on delivery of high quality and robust solutions to meet the most challenging environments. Our experts have vast experience within the North Sea Oil & Gas fields, an area with arguably the most demanding regulatory regime. We thrive on challenges and look forward to working with you to deliver efficient and effective solutions to your operations.


Our carefully selected partner organizations have delivered proven sustainable cost savings in various environments and we have combined their skillsets to allow us to deliver strategic pragmatic asset management solutions. All the tools we use are based on international industry standards including PAS 55 and JA101 and are compliant with the ISO 55000 set of standards as well as ISO 14224 taxonomy.


So whether you are looking for Vertical Startup at Project commissioning, Optimization of your Maintenance and/or Spares Strategy to improve Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) or improved Capital Asset Replacement Value (CARV/RAV), get in touch with our team of experts and we can work with you to devlop an effective solution.


Our ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Systems certification is on its way. Optima Asset Maintenance Solutions is registered in Scotland Registration Number SC511230.
