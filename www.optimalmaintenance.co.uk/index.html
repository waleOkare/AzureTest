Lean Asset Maintenance Activities

Our areas of specialisation include functional Criticality Analysis, Maintenance & Spares strategy builds/optimisation based on Reliability Centred Maintenance principles (SAE JA101), workload smoothing and asset data management. We work with asset intensive companies that are looking to manage risks and priorities given shrinking resources and rising costs. We deliver value through saving our Clients time and money whilst improving compliance, productivity and risk management in a diligent manner. Speak to us today if you are looking at ways to improve the quality and output from your maintenance planning systems and achieve higher efficiency in your maintenance processes.

We can offer unrivalled expertise in delivering services that are aligned to Client needs, creating value whilst ensuring that all mission critical asset risks are carefully managed. Get in touch today to discuss how we can apply our innovative cost saving solutions to your operations. Optimised Asset Maintenance Activities

Rising Operating Costs and declining margins mean that Planned and Corrective Maintenance activities have to be well co-ordinated. Too much Planned maintenance and your asset Availability is adversely affected - on the other hand too many breakdowns mean that Asset Reliability is low. Achieving and managing that fine balance is an art we specialise in here at Optima; get in touch and we can show you how!
