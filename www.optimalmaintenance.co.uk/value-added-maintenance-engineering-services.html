Cost Effective Asset Maintenance Solutions

Indepth Assessment of Asset Performance

Maintenance activities are seen as a necessary but costly activity. We work with you to determine the most necessary activities based on a robust Criticality Analysis and ensure that efforts are focused only on key activities. A key requirement for us undertaking a full Maintenance Optimisation is that we undertake a Site Visit to fully understand your Asset's operations.

Guaranteed Delivery on Schedule

We are fully aware of the importance of delivering on time as promised. Our philosophy of focusing on the final deliverable ensures that we are always working on what really matters and waste is reduced to a minimum. We promise to deliver your Project on time and on Schedule and we will work with you to evaluate and advise on the impact of any changes that may be required to the planning.

Relax and focus on your Core Activities

At Optima, we recognise that maintenance is merely the means to an end - your business is focused on your core deliverable which may be producing gas or oil. Our core deliverable is efficient and effective maintenance; and we can complement your operations to ensure you are focusing on what really matters.

Our Process

How we deliver Cost-Effective Maintenance Solutions

Understand your Operations

In order to deliver optimised Maintenance Solutions, we need to gain an understanding of your operations.

Site Visit

Where possible, we would like to undertake a Site Visit to gain a firsthand knowledge of your Operations.

Review OEM and Industry Guidelines

Depending on the complexity of the package we are reviewing, we undertake a full review of OEM requirements for maintenance along with the equipment's operating philosophy.

Benchmarking & Optimisation

Based on the Criticality of the Package, we then benchmark our findings against internal and external databases. We then present an optimised maintenance program for the package.

Asset Register Updates

All ageing Sites will have gone through modifications of one form or another. Keeping your Asset Register up to date is a tough task and our team can undertake a full review of your database to ensure its up to date.

Spare Parts Optimisation

No-one usually pays any attention to Spare Parts unless you have a breakdown and suddenly you realise that a critical part on a long lead time is not readily available. Our team can assist to ensure you have all critical Spares at hand.

Maintenance Engineering after Projects or Modifications

Most Projects are completed without the appropriate Spares and Maintenance in place. Here at Optima, we have a streamlined process that ensures that we keep track of your Project and deliver the required Spares and Maintenance at Project start-up.

Criticality Reviews

Maintenance & Spares are a huge overhead and ensuring that you are holding only the necessary Spares and undertaking only the required maintenance is key to efficient Operations. We ensure that you are fully compliant with the law at all times.

Manufacturer Warranty

Most equipment is delivered with specific recommendations from the Original Equipment Manufacturer to undertake specific activities at specific intervals to ensure compliance with Warranty conditions. We review all OEM documentation to ensure you are compliant at all times.

Optimised, Effective Maintenance Routines

We are familiar with a number of different ERM software and we can work with you to harness data from the field to ensure that you are organising your maintenance activities effectively. Predictive maintenance and other cutting edge techniques can provide effective maintenance management strategies.
