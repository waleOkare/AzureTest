Optima Asset Maintenance Solutions named Uberlytics UK Channel Partner

Tuesday, May 10, 2016      
AddThis Sharing Buttons
Share to TwitterShare to LinkedInShare to FacebookShare to RedditShare to EmailMore AddThis Share options
 

Optima Asset Maintenance Solutions, a maintenance engineering consultancy based in Aberdeen, Scotland, has reached a channel partnership agreement with Uberlytics LLC, internationally recognised experts in Functional System Criticality Analysis. The agreement extends Uberlytics reach to the UK and provides Optima with the latest technology in criticality and risk analysis.

Given the challenges faced by the UK Oil & Gas sector, this is a well-timed move that will enable Optima to offer state-of-the-art solutions to Asset Managers who have to decide how to manage an ever-growing asset work order backlog and competing priorities given ever shrinking resources and limited budgets. The solution, based on Uberlytics' Criticality Analyzer, software enables organisations to determine priorities based on a risk-based assessment against criteria that are vital to their business, which can include safety, regulatory compliance and production targets. The outcomes are fully auditable and visible to the entire organisation, which ensures a common understanding.

'We are very pleased to establish our first partnership in the UK with Optima Maintenance,' says Tacoma Zach, Uberlytics CEO. 'Accurately assessing criticality and risk is foundational to successful asset management. We look forward to supporting the Optima team as they deliver world-class criticality analysis solutions and lead their clients in establishing the right priorities for their maintenance programs.'
